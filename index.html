Artisanal Mastery: Hand Painter Protocol

  • Ensure Optimal Webcam Resolution for Enhanced Accuracy.
  • Maintain a Camera Distance of 40cm to 100cm for Optimal Results.
  • Ensure a Straight Palm Position for Improved Precision.
  • Commence Drawing by Closing All Fingers Except the Index Finger, or Simply Close the Thumb for Writing
  • Conclude Drawing by Straightening All Fingers, or Specifically Open the Thumb for Ceasing.
  • Utilize the Same Gesture to Select Buttons.
  • Choose the Clear Button to Instantly Erase the Screen.
  • The Default Color is White, and It Can Be Subsequently Changed to Red, Blue, or Green.
  • To Adjust the Brush Size, Hover Your Index Finger Over the Size Buttons Accordingly.
  • For Enhanced Accuracy, Position Your Full Palm at the Center of the Screen.
